Software Development

The Emerging Digital Academy is a 20 week intensive program teaching software development. Behind being a parent and a husband, it was the toughest commitment I’ve ever been through. The main takeaway: you will learn how to understand complicated material very quickly.

According to the EDA’s website, upon completion of the program, a student will spend over 800 hours studying, coding, presenting, communicating and working alongside fellow classmates. I didn’t believe that was an accurate number starting out, but I do now.
